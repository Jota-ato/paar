
# Paar - Two souls, one place.

Paar is a private space built for two — a calm, intentional app where couples can check in with each other beyond the noise of everyday messaging.

No group chats. No distractions. Just you and your partner.

---

## What Paar does

- 💛 **Mood check-ins** — Log how you're feeling each day and see how your partner is doing.
- 🙏 **Gratitude** — Send small thank-yous that don't get buried in a chat thread.
- 📝 **Shared notes** — A space for lists, ideas, and things that matter to both of you.
- 📸 **Memories** — Capture moments you don't want to forget.

---

## Why Paar exists

Meaningful things get lost in busy conversations. Paar gives couples a dedicated place to be honest, present, and connected — day by day.
